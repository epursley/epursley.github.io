The Dallas Cowboys officially became a franchised team on January 28, 1960. This was after the college draft which was the first time an expansion team played without the draft. However, it was not easy to become a franchise team. The redskins owner, George Preston Marshall made it quite difficult since he had a monopoly over AFC for decades. The way they got Marshall to allow the team to be franchised is by threating him by not allowing him to play "Hail to the Redskins"

In 1989 Jerry Jones became the owner of the Dallas Cowboys for $140 million. Right after he bought the team he fired Tom Landry and later on GM Tex Schramm.

The Cowboys have won 5 super bowls and Jones has been with the team for 3 of them. All of the Super Bowl wins will be on the next page.

In 2009, the construction of AT & T stadium was completed. The stadium cost $1.15 billion and can hold 80,000 fans and it the largest domed stadium in the world.
It also has a roof that can open and close which is pretty cool.

Dallas also has "The Star" which is the Cowboys $1.5 billion training facility, headquarters, indoor 12,000 seat multipurpose event center, restaurants, and 300 room Hotel.
