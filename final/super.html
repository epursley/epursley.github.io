Rings and Wins

Dallas has won 5 super bowls and have appeared there eight times.

Dallas Won their first super bowl in 1973 against the Dolphins winning 24-2.

There second win was in 1977 against the Broncos winning 27-10.

The third win was in 1992 against the Bills winning 52-17.

There fourth win was in 1993 against the Bills again winning 30-13.

There final win was against the steelers in 1995 with a score of 27-17.
